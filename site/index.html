Three moves from the menu bar.

No project files, no plugin chains, no waiting for a DAW to boot.

1

Set the tempo

Type a BPM, nudge it, or tap it in. Synclock becomes the heartbeat the whole rig follows.

2

Pick your gear

Every connected device shows up automatically. Choose what gets the clock, give it a nickname, and nudge its timing if it runs early or late. New gear stays silent until you say so.

3

Press play, or join Link

Send start and stop to your hardware, or follow an Ableton Link session so your laptop, apps, and gear all share the same beat.

Quiet, exact, out of the way.

You set a tempo and forget it's running. It only speaks up when something needs you.

Tight timing

The clock stays steady even when the Mac is busy, so the groove doesn't drift mid-set.

Works with any gear

Grooveboxes, synths, drum machines, interfaces. If it speaks MIDI, it shows up. Enable each one, nickname it, and dial in its timing.
